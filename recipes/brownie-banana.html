Brownie Banana Recipe

Description

Not all is chocolate!! , Banana and peanut butter brownie is totally delicious

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 3 eggs
  • 70g of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar or honey
  • 110g of wheat flour
  • chocolate butt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mash the bananas until smooth.
  3. Add eggs, peanut butter, and sugar/honey. Mix well.
  4. Stir in the flour until just combined.
  5. Fold in chocolate chunks if desired.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased baking dish.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let cool before cutting into squares.
